Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to common questions.

How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 2-5 business days within the US. Express shipping is available for $9.99 and delivers in 1-2 business days. You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.
What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be in their original packaging and in unused condition. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item.
Do you ship internationally?
We currently ship to the US, Canada, and EU countries. International shipping rates vary by destination and are calculated at checkout. Duties and taxes may apply depending on your location.
How do I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number and a link to the carrier's tracking page. You can also check order status from the account section on our website.
